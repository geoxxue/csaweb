Welcome

Welcome to the Chinese Student Association at University of Alaska Fairbanks.  As an important part of student organizations at UAF, we promotes the the interests and awareness of Chinese cultural to the whole community. Our activities are supported a lot by the International Office of UAF and University Baptist Church. In the past few years, many international friends and local residence have had fun with us. So you are very welcomed to join us and provide your suggestions to make us better developing even if you are not chinese or studying at UAF.
